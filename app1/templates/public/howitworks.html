Example journey

How a React learner and a UI/UX learner exchange value.

This example shows the platform in a real-world way: two users with opposite goals meet through the system, learn together, complete work, and improve through guided support.

Peer-to-peer skill exchange story

One user teaches React and wants to learn UI/UX design. Another teaches UI/UX and wants to learn React. Teach2Learn identifies the match and creates a structured collaboration path.

User A
Teaches React.js Learns UI/UX
↓ Matched through complementary goals
User B
Teaches UI/UX Learns React.js

Support features

More than matching — a full learning support system.

The platform is built to reduce friction after a match is created. Communication, progress tracking, interview preparation, and doubt clarification are all included in the same experience.

💬

Real-time chat

Users can discuss concepts, share files, send updates, and stay aligned between sessions.

📝

Assignments and notes

Mentors and peers can assign work, upload notes, review submissions, and provide feedback.

🤖

AI mock interviews

Skill-based practice questions and AI evaluation help users assess readiness and improve answers.

📈

Progress and certificates

Track completed sessions and assignments, then generate verified proof of learning milestones.
